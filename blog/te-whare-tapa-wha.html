Te Whare Tapa Whā

Wellbeing Plan - Based on The Four Walls of Maori Holistic Health

Taha Tiana (Physical Wellbeing)

  • Activities for Tiana (Physical) - these are the activities I will engage in to fulfill my need for physical wellbeing
    • Workout for 30 minutes every weekday morning
    • Tai Chi - Beijing 24 Short Form x2 every morning
    • Oneness Rhythmn Exercise - every morning
    • Walking daily, minimum of 3K
    • Hiking when possible on weekends - beach or track

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

  • Activities for Whānau (Family Wellbeing) - these are the activities I will engage in to fulfill my need for family/community wellbeing
    • Dinner with Sarah nightly
    • Hiking on the weekend when available with Sarah and friends

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

  • Activities for Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) - these are the activities I will engage in to fulfill my need for emotional and mental wellbeing
    • Nightly Ki no Kokyuho (Ki breathing) for 30 minutes

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

  • Activities for Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) - these are the activities I will engage in to fulfill my need for spiritual wellbeing
    • Nightly Ki no Kokyuho (Ki breathing) for 30 minutes
    • Daily recitation of the Serenity Prayer
    • Daily recitation of "Shinpo uchu rei kanno soku genjo" - "Trust in the universe and the universe will respond in kind" - to help me let go of things and attachments.

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

  • Activities for Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) - these are the activities I will engage in to fulfill my need for interconnections to the land and nature
    • Walking on the beach and getting in the water when warm enough every week
    • Walking daily, minimum of 3K
    • Hiking when possible on weekends - beach or track
